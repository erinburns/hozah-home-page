Why use Hozah?

Hozah is a fully automated parking system, which uses number plate recognition cameras to detect when you enter and leave a car park, and bill you accurately.

No need to carry cash

No waiting at the ticket machine

No need to download an app

Simply park and go

Need more reasons? Learn more

How it works

Drivers sign up to Hozah, providing number plate and payment details

ANPR cameras detect Hozah users entering and exiting car parks

Hozah automatically charges drivers for length-of-stay
